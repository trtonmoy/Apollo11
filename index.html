FAQ’s

Frequently Asked Questions

The Apollo 11 Token is a digital cryptocurrency meme token built on a blockchain platform (e.g., Ethereum, Binance Smart Chain, etc.). It is designed to serve various purposes within the Apollo 11 ecosystem, including but not limited to facilitating transactions, providing access to services, and potentially granting governance rights.

You can acquire Apollo 11 Tokens through various means, including participating in token sales, purchasing on cryptocurrency exchanges where it is listed, earning them through staking or rewards programs, or receiving them in airdrops or promotions.

The total supply of Apollo 11 Tokens is 1 Million, and it is fixed, meaning no additional tokens will ever be created.

You can store Apollo 11 Tokens in a compatible cryptocurrency wallet. Hardware wallets, software wallets, and even some exchange wallets (although less secure) can be used. It's essential to follow best practices for wallet security, such as enabling two-factor authentication and keeping your private keys secure.
